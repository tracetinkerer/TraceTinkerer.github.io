About Me

I'm Kay, a hands-on maker who spends my days working in a hardware store and her nights building things. Custom macropads, solder fume extractors, desk organisers and other 3d printed tools. I code in C++, Java, and HTML, and I love turning ideas into tangible, functional objects.

Philosophy

I believe in iterative design: build it, break it, build it better.
Whether it's a PCB layout or a 3D print, every version teaches me something new.

Hobbies

When I'm not soldering or modeling in CAD, I'm gaming on Steam, yapping on Discord, optimising my workspace, or tinkering with my 3D Printer. I love clever storage, compact homelab, and anything that makes my setup smarter.
